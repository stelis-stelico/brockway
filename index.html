Freshman Admission

Congratulations on considering Brockway for your college education! We welcome all freshmen who have recently graduated from high school or earned a GED. Our admission process is competitive, but we take a holistic approach to evaluating applications. We review academic performance, extracurricular activities, essays, and recommendations to get a full understanding of each applicant. We look forward to receiving your application and getting to know you better!

Transfer Admission

At Brockway, we welcome transfer students from other colleges and universities. We understand that transferring can be a big decision, and we want to make the process as smooth as possible. Our admission process evaluates each applicant's academic performance at their current institution, as well as any relevant work or volunteer experience. We also take into consideration the applicant's reason for transferring and their goals for their education. We are excited to see your application and learn more about your unique path to Brockway.

International Admission

Brockway is proud to welcome students from around the world to our campus. We believe that diversity and global perspectives enrich our community and our classrooms. Our international admission process considers the applicant's academic background, English proficiency, and any relevant standardized test scores. We also evaluate any cultural or extracurricular experiences that could contribute to our community. We offer resources to help international students adjust to college life in the United States, and we look forward to learning more about your unique background and goals.

Graduate Admission

Congratulations on considering graduate school at Brockway! Our graduate programs offer rigorous academic training and opportunities for research and professional development. Our admission process varies by program, but generally evaluates the applicant's academic performance, work or research experience, letters of recommendation, and personal statement. Some programs may also require standardized test scores or interviews. We are excited to receive your application and learn more about your goals for graduate study at Brockway.
